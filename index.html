About Me

I graduated from The University of Findlay in 2012 with a B.S. in Environmental, Safety and Occupational Health. After graduating started working at McWane Ductile as an Environmental Specialist, eventually being promoted to Assistant Manager of Environmental and Safety for its sister facility McWane Poles. From there I moved back home to be closer to family and accepted a position with Midway Products Group as an Environmental Engineer. Currently I am an Environmental Safety and Health Coordinator for Bridgestone APM Company and have been working their for three and a half years.

Throughout my experience in the ESH field I have been exposed to many different web applications that have asisted with my job. This exposure has gotten me interested in the web and software development field and I decided to take a Web Developer Boot Camp through The Ohio State University School of Engineering. I hope to combine the skills that I've aquired through my ESH career with the skills I'm learning in the Boot Camp to further the development of useful ESH software.
